Vega is a protocol for creating and trading derivatives on a fully decentralised network.

Fully automated processes and incentives for trading margined financial products allow for markets that are open and decentralised, with pseudonymous participants. Read about the concepts underpinning Vega on the blog.

Vega is proud to announce that we raised more than $5 million in a private seed round led by Pantera Capital, and supported by Xpring (Ripple), Hashed, NGC Ventures, gumi Cryptos Capital, Rockaway Blockchain, KR1, Eden Block, Focus Labs, Greenfield One, Monday Capital, RSK Ecosystem Fund and select private investors.

"We support the right to collaborate and trade freely. Privileged institutions and gatekeepers have for too long kept the financial system rigid and rigged in their favour. It is neither desirable nor necessary for market participants to be subject to profiteering, monitoring, and control by those privileged institutions." Read the full announcement.
